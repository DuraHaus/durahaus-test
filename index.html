The DuraHaus Method

A simple, field-tested approach for finishes that look better and last longer — across paint, sealants, drywall, and protective coatings.

DuraSpec • We inspect each surface and specify the right system for durability: primers, topcoats, enamels, sealants, or resurfacers.
DuraPrep • Surroundings protected. Substrates repaired, cleaned, sanded, or resurfaced to manufacturer spec.
DuraCoat • Correct films applied at proper mil thickness for the substrate and environment.
DuraCheck • Walkthrough with you, touch-ups, and a written workmanship warranty.
