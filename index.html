Painting & Coatings

Interior Painting

Crisp lines, smooth walls, and durable finishes for high-traffic spaces. We protect floors and furnishings, repair and sand surfaces, and apply pro-grade primers and enamels matched to each room. Perfect for walls, ceilings, trim, and cabinetry. Fast, tidy, and built to last.

Exterior Painting

Prep is everything outside. We address peeling, chalking, and failed caulk; repair substrates; then prime and coat with weather-rated systems designed for Midwest freeze/thaw. Expect strong adhesion, even color, and clean details on siding, brick, trim, doors, and more.

Concrete Coatings

What We Do — More Ways We Help

Caulking & Sealants

Airtight, watertight joints that make paint last longer. We remove failing caulk, clean and dry surfaces, and install premium sealants matched to the substrate and movement. Cleaner lines, better energy performance, and fewer callbacks.

Learn more

Wood Repair & Replacement

Rotting or damaged trim invites water and ruins the look. We cut out rot, rebuild with epoxy where appropriate, or replace with the right materials, then prime and coat for a seamless finish. Proper repair ensures your paint system performs for years.

Learn more

Pressure Washing

Proper cleaning is the foundation of great results. We remove dirt, chalk, mildew, and loose coatings with the right detergents and pressure, protecting landscaping and adjacent surfaces. Essential prep for lasting adhesion.

Learn more

Metal Coatings

Metal buildings, rails, and trim need specific prep and coatings. We clean and treat rust, select compatible primers (including DTM/industrial systems), and apply finishes designed for UV and corrosion resistance. A sharper look with real protection.

FAQs

Do you offer free estimates?
Yes. We can visit on-site quickly in most cases and provide a written, itemized quote.
How soon can you start?
Scheduling depends on scope and season. Smaller interior projects can often begin within 1–2 weeks.
How do you prepare surfaces?
We clean, repair, sand, and prime as needed, then apply coatings matched to the substrate and environment. Proper prep is the key to durability.
What materials do you use?
Pro-grade primers, enamels, and exterior coatings chosen for adhesion, coverage, and weather resistance. We’ll specify products on your quote.
How do you protect my home?
We cover floors and furnishings, mask as needed, protect landscaping, and keep a tidy jobsite with daily cleanup.
Do you help with colors and finishes?
Yes—sheen and color affect look and durability. We can recommend options that fit your lighting and lifestyle.
Are you insured? Do you offer a warranty?
Yes—fully insured. Every project includes a workmanship warranty. Details are included on your quote.
What about payment and scheduling?
We schedule to minimize disruption and keep you informed. Typical terms include a deposit and final payment upon walkthrough.
