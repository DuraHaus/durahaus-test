Quick finish cheat-sheet

Pick the right sheen Flat for ceilings, matte/eggshell for walls, satin/semi-gloss for trim & doors. Right sheen = fewer touch-ups and a cleaner look.
Primer really matters Bonding for slick cabinets/metal, stain-blocking for repairs/knots, masonry primers for brick/block. Better adhesion, better color.
Seal joints the right way Use backer rod + movement-rated sealant in wider gaps; paintable acrylic-urethane where finish matters.
Exterior longevity Sound prep + flexible joints + UV-stable topcoat. That trio is what resists peel and fade.
