FAQs

How fast can you quote?
Most quotes are turned around within 24–48 hours after a quick walkthrough.
What’s included in prep?
Masking, protection, sanding/scuffing, gap/void caulking, patch/repair, spot/bonding primer, and substrate-specific primers as needed.
How long do projects take?
Small interiors: 1–3 days. Full exteriors vary by size + repairs. Cabinets are typically 4–7 days depending on scope and drying windows.
Do you help choose colors and finishes?
Yes. We can suggest sheens and product families based on the surface and traffic, and we can coordinate with your designer if you have one.
What products do you use?
We match the system to the surface: cabinet enamels/urethanes, elastomeric exteriors, bonding/adhesion primers, stain blockers, rust-inhibitives for metal, and moisture-tolerant masonry coatings.
Payment & scheduling?
We schedule a start window at approval. A deposit may be due at scheduling; balance on completion after walkthrough.
Are you insured? Do you warranty?
Yes—fully insured. Every project includes a written workmanship warranty.
