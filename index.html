What We Do

Premium prep + matched coating systems for homes across Butler, Hamilton, and Warren County.

Interior Painting

Walls, ceilings, trim, doors, and built-ins finished with low-odor, washable coatings. We protect floors and furniture, repair dings, sand between coats, and leave crisp lines and a clean site.

Exterior Painting

Power-wash, scrape, sand, spot-prime, and caulk—then apply weather-tough topcoats. We match products to siding (wood, fiber-cement, aluminum) and handle fascia/soffit repairs as needed.

Concrete / Masonry Coatings

Garages, basements, and patios prepped and coated with moisture-tolerant primers and abrasion-resistant finishes. Options for anti-slip texture and chemical resistance where you need it.

Cabinet Painting / Refinishing

Factory-smooth results using degrease → sand → adhesion-prime → tinted enamel/urethane. Doors and drawers sprayed for a uniform sheen; boxes masked and finished dust-free.

Staining & Wood Finishes

Decks, doors, and rails cleaned, neutralized, and finished with transparent, semi-transparent, or solid stains. We advise on color and UV protection to keep the grain looking great longer.

Drywall Repair

Cracks, nail pops, water spots, and full patch-ins blended to invisible. We prime repairs to lock down texture and ensure a seamless sheen after paint.

Caulking / Sealant Application

Interior gaps and exterior joints sealed with paintable elastomeric sealants for air/water control and a cleaner finished line around trim, windows, and doors.

Wood Repair / Replacement

Rot removal, trim/fascia replacement, and epoxy rebuilds where appropriate—primed and painted as part of a complete system so repairs disappear into the finish.

Pressure Washing

Surface prep for paint and stain as well as stand-alone cleaning. We use the right tips, detergents, and pressure to lift dirt without damaging siding or concrete.

Metal Coatings

Rust treatment, etch/prime, and industrial topcoats for rails, doors, and siding panels. We specify rust-inhibitive systems to keep steel looking sharp and protected.

FAQs

How fast can you quote?
Most quotes are turned around within 24–48 hours after a quick walkthrough.
What’s included in prep?
Masking/protection, sanding or de-glossing, gap/void caulking, patch/repair, spot/bonding primer, and substrate-specific primers as needed.
How long do projects take?
Small interiors: 1–3 days. Full exteriors vary by size + repairs. Cabinets are typically 4–7 days depending on scope and drying windows.
Do you help choose colors and finishes?
Yes. We suggest sheens and product families based on the surface and traffic, and we can coordinate with your designer if you have one.
What products do you use?
We match the system to the surface: cabinet enamels/urethanes, elastomeric exteriors, bonding/adhesion primers, stain blockers, rust-inhibitives for metal, and moisture-tolerant masonry coatings.
Are you insured? Do you warranty?
Yes—fully insured, with a written workmanship warranty.
